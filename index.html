Our Heritage

Cooking Up Excellence Since 1985

What started as a small family dream has grown into a barbecue destination that honors tradition while embracing innovation. Our founder, Big Jim Thompson, began smoking meats in his backyard, perfecting recipes that would become the foundation of Smokehouse BBQ.

Today, our award-winning pitmasters continue this legacy, starting each day before dawn to tend our custom-built smokers. Great barbecue can't be rushed — it's an art form requiring patience, skill, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Craft & Method

Our Smoking Process

From selection to serving — every step orchestrated for perfection

01

Premium Selection

Hand-selected cuts from trusted local suppliers, ensuring marbling and quality above all.

02

Signature Seasoning

Each cut coated generously with our secret 12-spice dry rub and cured overnight.

03

Low & Slow Smoking

Smoked at 225°F using a blend of oak, hickory, and fruitwoods for 12–16 hours.

04

Perfect Service

Carved to order and served fresh from our smokers with house-made sauces and sides.

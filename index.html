Our Heritage

A story of passion, tradition, and the relentless pursuit of barbecue perfection

Cooking Up Excellence Since 1985

What started as a small family dream has grown into a barbecue destination that honors tradition while embracing innovation. Our founder, Big Jim Thompson, began smoking meats in his backyard, perfecting recipes that would eventually become the foundation of Smokehouse BBQ.

Today, our award-winning pitmasters continue this legacy, starting each day before dawn to tend our custom-built smokers. We believe that great barbecue can't be rushed – it's an art form that requires patience, skill, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

From our signature dry rubs made with 12 secret spices to our hand-selected hardwoods, every element is carefully chosen to create the perfect barbecue experience. This dedication has earned us recognition as one of the finest BBQ establishments in the region.

Our Smoking Process

From selection to serving, every step is carefully orchestrated for barbecue perfection

1

Premium Selection

We hand-select only the finest cuts from trusted local suppliers, ensuring marbling and quality.

2

Signature Seasoning

Each cut is generously coated with our secret 12-spice dry rub and allowed to cure overnight.

3

Low & Slow Smoking

Meats are smoked at 225°F using a blend of oak, hickory, and fruitwoods for 12-16 hours.

4

Perfect Service

Carved to order and served fresh from our smokers with house-made sauces and classic sides.
